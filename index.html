Pollen Forecast Webcal

You can get the pollen forecast for your location directly into your calendar. This webcal uses emojis to display a 5 days forecast of various pollens. Enter your city, select pollens and adjust your preferences the get the feed. Works with all calendars supporting online .ics and emojis, like Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook on iOS, Android, MacOS and Windows.

Add to your Calendar 

            webcal://pollencal.com/?location=Ditzingen&pollen=grass
